As a student at Brigham Young University, I am determined to never stop learning and growing. My dad always lectured his kids growing up that we must "contribute more than we consume," and that has become my personal mission in life: as a friend, student, and co-worker.
My passion for business and marketing is fed through the fast-paced Marketing Society and Women in Business clubs that I am a part of. I am on track to graduate with my bachelor's in marketing in the Spring of 2019. As VP of Analytics in the Marketing Association, I have been able to develop statistical skills that help me make data-driven decisions and as the Leader of Technology in the Women in Business club, I have seen the social and impacts that equality in the workplace can make.
Outside of school, I enjoy working on production and design skills. I love art and like to work on both my creative skills and technical skills using Adobe Creative Cloud. From coding to design, I try to find passion in what I do so that I will contribute more than I consume.
